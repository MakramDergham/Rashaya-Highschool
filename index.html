Rashaya Highschool

General Overview

Located at the heart of Rashaya Al Wadi, Rashaya Highschool is a prominent educational institution offering a wide variety of acedemic subjects and strong educational levels. Over the past years, this Highschool played a dominant role in extracurricular activities, combining community service with fun experiences, therefore contributing in the making of a well-educated student.

Notable sites

Where education meets innovation and nature. Each site is designed to inspire and engage, offering unique learning experiences across various disciplines. From cutting-edge laboratories to lush, vibrant gardens, our Highschool is a place of discovery and exploration. Here, students not only learn theoretical knowledge but also apply it in practical, real-world environments. Join us on a journey through these special spaces, each crafted to foster curiosity, creativity, and a deeper understanding of the world.

Chemistry Lab

Explore the world of molecules and reactions in our Chemistry Lab. This facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure a safe and enriching experience for students and teachers.

STEAM Lab

Dive into an immersive, hands-on learning environment where creativity meets technology. Our STEAM Lab fosters innovation with cutting-edge tools and projects that prepare students for the challenges of tomorrow.

RHS Garden

Explore our student-cultivated garden, Our students take pride in nurturing an array of regional plants such as za'atar, known for its distinctive flavor and culinary uses; lavender, celebrated for its soothing aroma and therapeutic properties; and a variety of medicinal herbs. The garden also features bee hives, integral to our natural beekeeping and honey production educational program
