Rashaya Highschool

Annual Events

Rashaya Highschool holds various extracurricular events annually, ranging from sports tournaments and academic competitions to festivals and community service projects. These events not only foster a sense of friendship among students but also provide opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, and the exploration of diverse interests beyond the classroom.

Open Day

Open Day is a yearly event held in Rashaya Highschool, combining all types of fun activities listing from basketball, trivia games, cooking competitions and many more. This event aims at discovering the hidden talents of students, and making unforgettable memories along with teachers and friends.

Christmas Market

Our school's Christmas market is a festive gathering where twinkling lights, delicious treats, and handmade crafts create a joyful atmosphere. It's a cherished event where students, teachers, and families come together to celebrate the season, share gifts, and support charitable causes.

Rally Paper

The Rashaya Rally Paper is a thrilling adventure where teams explore the scenic wonders of the region, solving clues and completing challenges along the way. It's a journey filled with excitement, camaraderie, and unforgettable discoveries, showcasing the beauty of Rashaya and fostering lasting memories.

Hiking

Rashaya High School's hiking trips immerse students in nature's beauty, offering invigorating trails and breathtaking views. These adventures foster a deep connection with the outdoors, creating lasting memories of exploration and discovery.
